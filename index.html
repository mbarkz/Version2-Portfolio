ABOUT

Hey! That’s me!

This image of me could not be in more stark contrast to my current reality; cold, wet and dark in the UK’s winter time. Oh well, it’s not all bad. Thankfully I have the EdEx Front End Web Development Boot Camp to see me through the winter. In fact you’re currently looking at my homework, no copying please! Only kidding.

This is an update to my portfolio page. Originally, I had hand typed each and every letter of the HTML and CSS, wow what a fool I was! On this one, I used a nifty framework called Bootstrap. In doing so, it allowed me to speed up the process and gave me the time to get a little more creative with the design of the web page. Luckily too for you mobile readers, the page is much more responsive, and I didn’t even have to do too much work to make it so!

For a lack of any better images, I’ve added some pics of my trip around Spain on my bike last year. I cycled from Santander to Barcelona, living out of my tent and hitting the road each and every day. Something I hope this Front End Web course allows me to do more of by giving me more flexibility to do away with pesky employers when they say no to my annual leave requests. Maybe one day I’ll do away with them for good :).

Thanks for taking the time to look at my portfolio.
